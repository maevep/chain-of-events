Chain of Events

By Maeve Potter

The toddler's tooth falls out. It hits the floor and bounces into a cup of water. The mother drinks the water. The mother chokes on the tooth. The mother coughs up the tooth and it flies out the window. The tooth hits a man in the eye and he stumbles into the street. A passing car narrowly avoids the man, crashing into a street lamp.
